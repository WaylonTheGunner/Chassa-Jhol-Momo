Our Story

Crafted with Authentic
Nepali Passion

Chassa Jhol Mo:Mo is dedicated to serving authentic Nepali momo with premium quality ingredients, exceptional hygiene, and unforgettable taste. From freshly prepared steamed momo to our signature homemade achar and flavorful jhol, we deliver a rich culinary experience with fast and reliable service.

Every dumpling is hand-folded with care, filled with fresh ingredients, and served with our secret jhol broth — a recipe perfected over years of culinary dedication. Whether you visit us or order delivery, you get the same restaurant-quality experience.
